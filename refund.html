Refund Policy

1. Refund Eligibility

We offer refunds only if you face problems with our service and if the class has not yet started or is about to start within the next 2 days. Refunds are not possible once the class has started.

2. How to Apply for a Refund

If you wish to apply for a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Enter your Phone Number.
  2. Provide your Name.
  3. Include your Email or Gmail address.
  4. Attach a Screenshot of the payment. Ensure all information is correct, as incorrect details may result in a denial of the refund and necessitate resubmission.

Please email all the details to refund@soulfulltrader.xyz.

3. Important Notes

Ensure that you provide accurate information and attach the correct screenshot file. Refunds will be processed to the same account from which the payment was made. Please allow up to 7-10 days for the refund to be completed, depending on our payment partner.
